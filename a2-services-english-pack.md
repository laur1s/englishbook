# A2 Everyday Services Pack
## A2 kasdienių paslaugų anglų kalba

Šiame rinkinyje yra 14 dažnų paslaugų situacijų. Kiekvienoje mokomasi aiškiai pasakyti faktus, mandagiai paprašyti, pasitikslinti ir susitarti dėl konkretaus kito žingsnio.

### Universali paslaugų pokalbio formulė

1. **Open / Pradėkite:** *Hello. I'm calling about... / I'd like to...*
2. **Identify / Nurodykite:** vardas, užsakymas, vizitas ar prekė.
3. **Explain / Paaiškinkite:** vienas ar du patikrinami faktai.
4. **Request / Paprašykite:** *Could you...? Is it possible to...?*
5. **Confirm / Patvirtinkite:** laikas, kaina, vieta arba kitas veiksmas.
6. **Close / Užbaikite:** *Thank you for checking.*

## Service 1 — Booking an Appointment / Registracija

**Phrase bank**

| English | Lietuviška reikšmė |
|---|---|
| I'd like to make an appointment. | Norėčiau užsiregistruoti. |
| Is there anything available on Monday? | Ar pirmadienį yra laisvų laikų? |
| Morning would be better for me. | Man labiau tiktų rytas. |
| Could you confirm the time? | Ar galėtumėte patvirtinti laiką? |

**Model:** *I'd like to make an appointment for an eye test. Is there anything available on Monday morning? Ten thirty works for me. Could you confirm the address, please?*

**Repair:** 1. *I want register.* 2. *Have free time Monday?* 3. *Confirm hour please.*

**Your task:** pasirinkite paslaugą, dvi galimas dienas ir tinkamą dienos dalį.  
**Checklist:** paslauga; diena; laiko pageidavimas; galutinis patvirtinimas.

## Service 2 — Changing an Appointment / Vizito keitimas

**Model dialogue**

> **Customer:** Hello. I have an appointment on Tuesday at two, but I need to change it.  
> **Receptionist:** Is another day better?  
> **Customer:** Yes. Would Thursday afternoon be possible?  
> **Receptionist:** We have 15:30.  
> **Customer:** That works. So the new appointment is Thursday at 15:30, correct?

**Repair:** 1. *I need change appointment.* 2. *Thursday afternoon is possible?* 3. *New appointment on 15:30.*

**Your task:** pakeiskite dieną, laiką arba paslaugos trukmę ir pakartokite naują informaciją.  
**Checklist:** sena informacija; pakeitimo priežastis nebūtina išsamiai; naujas variantas; patvirtinimas.

## Service 3 — At a Hairdresser / Kirpykloje

**Phrase bank:** *I'd like a trim; just a little shorter; keep the length at the front; could you show me the back?; How much will it cost?*

**Model:** *I'd like a trim, please. Could you make it a little shorter at the back but keep the length at the front? I don't want a big change. How much will it cost?*

**Choose the natural phrase:**

1. **Cut me hair. / I'd like a haircut, please.**
2. **Make little shorter. / Could you make it a little shorter?**
3. **How many costs? / How much will it cost?**

**Your task:** apibūdinkite norimą kirpimą arba kitą paprastą grožio paslaugą.  
**Checklist:** paslauga; du konkretūs pageidavimai; ko nenorite; kaina.

---

## Service 4 — Asking at a Pharmacy / Vaistinėje

**Safe structure:** simptomas → trukmė → svarbus faktas → klausimas → kada kreiptis į specialistą.

**Model:** *I've had a mild headache since this morning. I have eaten and drunk water, but it hasn't improved. I take regular medication, so I would like to check what may be suitable. When should I speak to a doctor?*

**Repair:** 1. *I have headache from morning.* 2. *It didn't improved.* 3. *What medicine I must take?*

**Your task:** sukurkite pokalbį apie paprastą negalavimą neprašydami diagnozės.  
**Checklist:** *for/since*; svarbi informacija; prašymas paaiškinti galimybes; profesionalios pagalbos klausimas.

**Svarbu:** esant stipriems, staigiems ar nerimą keliantiems simptomams, remkitės vietos skubios pagalbos nurodymais, o ne kalbos pratimu.

## Service 5 — At Clinic Reception / Klinikos registratūroje

**Information card**

- name: Rūta Vaitkutė
- appointment: Dr Brown, 09:40
- reason at reception: check in
- additional need: explain where to wait

**Your sentence plan:** *I have an appointment with... at... My name is... Do I need to...? Where should I...?*

**Model:** *Good morning. I have an appointment with Dr Brown at 09:40. My name is Rūta Vaitkutė. Do I need to complete any forms? Where should I wait?*

**Repair:** 1. *I have meeting with Dr Brown.* 2. *It is on 09:40.* 3. *Where I should wait?*

**Checklist:** *appointment with*; *at + time*; vardas; du registratūros klausimai.

## Service 6 — Posting a Parcel / Siuntos išsiuntimas

**Parcel facts:** destination—Germany; weight—1.4 kg; contents—books; need—tracking; preference—cheapest option under ten days.

**Model dialogue**

> **Customer:** I'd like to send this parcel to Germany.  
> **Clerk:** Standard or express delivery?  
> **Customer:** What is the cheapest tracked option that takes less than ten days?  
> **Clerk:** Standard tracked delivery costs €14.  
> **Customer:** That's fine. Where should I write the return address?

**Repair:** 1. *Send parcel until Germany.* 2. *What is most cheap option?* 3. *Where write return address?*

**Your task:** pakeiskite šalį, turinį, terminą ir pristatymo prioritetą.  
**Checklist:** destination; contents described truthfully; tracking; price or time; address question.

---

## Service 7 — A Late Delivery / Vėluojanti siunta

**Tracking history**

| Date | Status |
|---|---|
| 3 June | order dispatched |
| 4 June | arrived at local depot |
| 5 June | delivery delayed |
| 7 June | no new update |

**Questions:** 1. When was it dispatched? 2. Where did it arrive on 4 June? 3. How many days have passed without a new update by 7 June? 4. Which fact should the customer mention first?

**Model request:** *My order has been at the local depot since 4 June, and there has been no new update for two days. Could you check the expected delivery date and email me today?*

**Your task:** 45–60 žodžių žinutė su užsakymo numeriu, faktais ir konkrečiu prašymu.  
**Checklist:** be kaltinimų; datos; dabartinė būsena; norimas atnaujinimas.

---

## Service 8 — A Missing Parcel Part / Trūkstama siuntos dalis

**Situation:** lentynoje turi būti A, B, C ir D dalys. Siuntoje nėra C dalies; dėžė nepažeista; turite nuotrauką ir kvitą.

**Model email:** *Hello, I received order 8821 today. The box was not damaged, but part C is missing, so I cannot assemble the shelf. I have checked all the packaging and attached a photo and receipt. Could you send part C by express delivery, please?*

**Repair:** 1. *Part C is missed.* 2. *I checked all packagings.* 3. *Can send missing detail?*

**Your task:** pakeiskite daiktą, trūkstamą dalį ir reikalingą terminą.  
**Checklist:** užsakymas; tikslus trūkumas; kas patikrinta; įrodymas; tinkamas sprendimas.

---

## Service 9 — Returning a Product / Prekės grąžinimas

**Receipt facts:** kettle, bought 12 May, €32, card payment. Problem: switches off after ten seconds. Customer tried another socket.

**Model:** *I'd like to return this kettle. I bought it on 12 May and paid by card. It switches off after about ten seconds, even in another socket. I have the receipt. Could I have a replacement, please?*

**Choose:**

1. paid **with / by** card
2. bought it **in / on** 12 May
3. switches **off / out** after ten seconds
4. could I **have / get me** a replacement

**Your task:** pasirinkite kitą prekę, matomą gedimą, bandytą veiksmą ir norimą sprendimą.  
**Checklist:** pirkimo data; mokėjimas; faktinis gedimas; bandymas; kvitas; sprendimas.

## Service 10 — A Repair Visit / Meistro vizitas

**Model phone call**

> **Customer:** I'm calling about tomorrow's washing-machine repair. Could you confirm the arrival window?  
> **Agent:** The engineer will arrive between 10 and 12.  
> **Customer:** Please ask them to call twenty minutes before arriving. The entrance is at the back of the building.  
> **Agent:** Certainly. Is there parking nearby?  
> **Customer:** Yes, there is a visitor space beside the gate.

**Repair:** 1. *Confirm coming time.* 2. *Call before twenty minutes.* 3. *Entrance is from back.*

**Your task:** susitarkite dėl laiko intervalo, skambučio prieš atvykstant ir įėjimo ar parkavimo.  
**Checklist:** paslauga; data; intervalas; kontaktas; patekimo instrukcija.

## Service 11 — A Billing Question / Klausimas dėl sąskaitos

**Bill extract**

| Item | Expected | Charged |
|---|---:|---:|
| monthly service | €24 | €24 |
| equipment rental | €0 | €8 |
| one-time installation | €15 | €15 |
| total | €39 | €47 |

**Questions:** 1. Which charge is unexpected? 2. What should the total be without it? 3. What exact question should the customer ask?

**Model:** *I'm calling about invoice 419. It includes an €8 equipment-rental charge, but my agreement says the equipment is included. Could you explain this charge and correct the invoice if it is a mistake?*

**LT pagalba:** finansinėje situacijoje nepateikite jautrių kortelės duomenų. Nurodykite sąskaitos numerį ir konkretų eilutės neatitikimą.

## Service 12 — A Restaurant Bill Problem / Klaida restorano sąskaitoje

**Ordered:** two soups, one pasta, one salad, two waters.  
**Bill:** two soups, **two pasta dishes**, one salad, two waters.

**Model dialogue**

> **Customer:** Excuse me, I think there is a small mistake on the bill.  
> **Server:** What seems to be wrong?  
> **Customer:** We ordered one pasta dish, but the bill includes two. Could you check it, please?  
> **Server:** Of course. I'll correct it now.  
> **Customer:** Thank you.

**Repair:** 1. *In bill is mistake.* 2. *We ordered one pasta, but wrote two.* 3. *Check it.*

**Your task:** sukurkite kitą sąskaitos neatitikimą ir ramiai jį išspręskite.  
**Checklist:** mandagi pradžia; užsakyta informacija; sąskaitos informacija; prašymas; padėka.

---

## Service 13 — A Noise Complaint / Skundas dėl triukšmo

**Model call:** *Hello, I'm staying in room 214. There has been loud music in the room next door for about an hour, and it is now after midnight. Could you contact the room or help me move to a quieter room? I have an early train tomorrow.*

**Why this works:** nurodyta vieta, trukmė, dabartinis laikas, poveikis ir du realūs sprendimai.

**Repair:** 1. *Music plays since one hour.* 2. *Now is after midnight.* 3. *Can change me room?*

**Your task:** pasirinkite viešbutį arba daugiabutį; pateikite tik faktus ir pagrįstą prašymą.  
**Checklist:** vieta; trukmė arba pradžios laikas; poveikis; ramus tonas; sprendimas.

---

## Service 14 — Full Complaint and Follow-Up / Visas skundas ir atsakymas

**Situation:** užsakėte stalinę lempą; pristatyta pavėluotai; laidas pažeistas; prekės naudoti nesaugu; turite nuotrauką; norite pakeitimo arba pinigų grąžinimo.

**Write 70–90 words using:**

1. subject line;
2. order and delivery facts;
3. exact problem;
4. what evidence you have;
5. requested solution;
6. time expectation;
7. polite close.

**Model:**  
> **Subject: Damaged lamp — order 5638**  
> Hello, I received order 5638 today, three days later than the confirmed delivery date. The lamp's cable is damaged, so the product is not safe to use. I have attached a clear photo of the cable and my receipt. Could you send a safe replacement, or provide a full refund if no replacement is available? Please confirm the next step within two working days. Kind regards, Asta

**Follow-up speaking task:** klientų aptarnavimo darbuotojas siūlo tik nuolaidą. Paaiškinkite, kodėl tai neišsprendžia saugumo problemos.  
**Checklist:** faktai; saugumo problema be dramatizavimo; įrodymas; du priimtini sprendimai; terminas.

# Services Answer and Repair Key / Atsakymai

## Service 1
1. I'd like to **make an appointment**. 2. **Is there anything available on Monday?** 3. Could you confirm **the time**, please?

## Service 2
1. I need **to** change **my/the** appointment. 2. **Would Thursday afternoon be possible?** 3. The new appointment is **at** 15:30.

## Service 3
1. I'd like a haircut, please. 2. Could you make it a little shorter? 3. How much will it cost?

## Service 4
1. I have **had a** headache **since this morning**. 2. It **hasn't improved**. 3. *What options may be suitable?* / *When should I speak to a doctor?* Venkite savarankiškai reikalauti konkretaus vaisto be profesionalaus patarimo.

## Service 5
1. I have **an appointment** with Dr Brown. 2. It is **at** 09:40. 3. Where **should I** wait?

## Service 6
1. I'd like to send this parcel **to** Germany. 2. What is **the cheapest** option? 3. Where **should I write the** return address?

## Service 7
1. 3 June. 2. At the local depot. 3. Two days. 4. The parcel has been at the depot since 4 June and there is no new update.

## Service 8
1. Part C is **missing**. 2. I checked all **the packaging**. 3. Could you send the missing **part**?

## Service 9
1. by 2. on 3. off 4. have.

## Service 10
1. Could you confirm **the arrival time/window**? 2. Please call **twenty minutes before arriving**. 3. The entrance is **at the back**.

## Service 11
1. The €8 equipment-rental charge. 2. €39. 3. *Could you explain the €8 equipment-rental charge and correct the invoice if it is a mistake?*

## Service 12
1. There is **a mistake on the** bill. 2. We ordered one pasta dish, but the bill **includes** two. 3. Could you check it, please?

## Service 13
1. The music **started an hour ago and is still playing**. 2. It is now after midnight. 3. Could you move **me to another room**?

Atvirų užduočių atsakymai skirsis. Tikrinkite, ar prašymas konkretus, tonas ramus, o sprendimas atitinka problemą. Po kiekvieno taisymo sukurkite naują sakinį su savo tikra data, laiku, preke ar paslauga.
