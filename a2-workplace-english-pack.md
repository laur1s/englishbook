# A2 Workplace English Pack
## A2 anglų kalba darbe

Šis rinkinys skirtas kasdienėms darbo situacijoms. Tikslas—ne oficiali verslo anglų kalba, o aiškus A2 bendravimas: prisistatyti, suprasti grafiką, parašyti trumpą žinutę, paskambinti, pasitikslinti, pranešti apie problemą ir susitarti dėl kito žingsnio.

### Aiškios darbo žinutės formulė

**Context → fact → action → time → polite close**  
**Kontekstas → faktas → veiksmas → laikas → mandagi pabaiga**

> *I'm writing about tomorrow's delivery. The driver will arrive one hour late. Please keep the loading area free until 11:30. Could you confirm that this works? Thank you.*

## Workday 1 — First-Day Introductions / Pirma diena darbe

| English | Lietuviška reikšmė |
|---|---|
| I'm the new assistant in the logistics team. | Esu naujas logistikos komandos asistentas. |
| What are you responsible for? | Už ką esate atsakingas? |
| I mainly deal with customer orders. | Daugiausia dirbu su klientų užsakymais. |
| Who should I ask about the schedule? | Kieno klausti apie grafiką? |

**Model:** *Hi, I'm Milda, the new assistant in the logistics team. I mainly deal with customer orders and delivery messages. What are you responsible for? Also, who should I ask about next week's schedule?*

**Quick repair:** 1. *I am new assistant.* 2. *For what you are responsible?* 3. *I deal about orders.*

**Your task:** 5–6 sakiniais prisistatykite naujam kolegai.  
**Checklist:** vardas; pareigos; du darbai; vienas klausimas; draugiška pabaiga.

## Workday 2 — Reading a Schedule / Darbo grafikas

**Schedule**

| Time | Task | Place |
|---|---|---|
| 08:30 | team briefing | meeting room 2 |
| 09:00–11:00 | customer emails | main office |
| 11:15 | delivery check | warehouse |
| 12:30 | lunch | — |
| 14:00 | safety training | online |

**Questions:** 1. What happens first? 2. How long is the email block? 3. Where is the delivery check? 4. Is training before or after lunch? 5. Which task is online?

**Language:** *The briefing starts at...; Email work lasts for...; Training is after...; I have to be in...*

**Your task:** paaiškinkite grafiką kolegai, kuris jo nemato.  
**Checklist:** visi penki įvykiai; tikslus laikas; vieta; *before/after*.

## Workday 3 — A Short Team Message / Trumpa komandos žinutė

**Too vague / Per neaišku:** *Meeting changed. Come later.*  
**Clear / Aišku:** *Today's team meeting has moved from 2:00 to 2:30. It will still be in meeting room 2. Please tell me if the new time causes a problem.*

**Repair choices:**

1. Best subject: **Thing / Team meeting moved to 2:30 / Hello**
2. Best time phrase: **on 2:30 / at 2:30 / in 2:30**
3. Best check: **Problem? / Does the new time work for you? / You come.**

**Your task:** parašykite 35–50 žodžių žinutę: mokymai perkeliami iš trečiadienio ryto į ketvirtadienį 15:00, vieta nesikeičia.  
**Model starter:** *Hi everyone, this week's training has moved...*  
**Checklist:** kas keičiasi; senas ir naujas laikas; vieta; prašymas patvirtinti.

## Workday 4 — A Useful Email / Naudingas el. laiškas

**Email model**

> **Subject: Friday stock check**  
> Hi Tomas,  
> I'm writing about Friday's stock check. I have completed sections A and B, but section C is not ready because two delivery lists are missing. Could you send them by Thursday at 3 p.m.? Then I can finish the check before Friday morning.  
> Thanks,  
> Rasa

**Notice:** aiški tema, atlikta dalis, kliūtis, konkretus prašymas, terminas ir rezultatas.

**Quick repair:** 1. *I write about Friday.* 2. *Two lists is missing.* 3. *Send until Thursday.*

**Your task:** parašykite el. laišką apie nebaigtą užduotį, nes trūksta vienos informacijos dalies.  
**Checklist:** temos eilutė; kas padaryta; ko trūksta; prašymas; *by + deadline*.

## Workday 5 — Answering a Work Call / Darbo skambutis

| Function | Phrase |
|---|---|
| answer | *Good morning, North Office. Lina speaking.* |
| ask identity | *May I ask who's calling?* |
| person unavailable | *I'm afraid he's in a meeting.* |
| offer action | *Can I take a message?* |
| check detail | *Could you spell the surname, please?* |

**Model dialogue**

> **A:** Good morning, North Office. Lina speaking.  
> **B:** Hello. Could I speak to Mr Valeika?  
> **A:** I'm afraid he's in a meeting. Can I take a message?  
> **B:** Yes, please ask him to call Nora Bell about order 613.  
> **A:** Could you spell the surname, please?

**Quick repair:** 1. *Lina speaks.* 2. *Who calls?* 3. *He is on meeting.*

**Your task:** suvaidinkite skambutį: asmuo nepasiekiamas, reikia vardo, numerio, temos ir perskambinimo laiko.  
**Checklist:** profesionali pradžia; keturios žinutės detalės; viena detalė pakartota patikrinimui.

## Workday 6 — A Simple Meeting Update / Trumpas susitikimo pranešimas

**Four-part update:**

1. *I've completed...*—kas padaryta.
2. *I'm working on...*—kas vyksta dabar.
3. *The main problem is...*—kliūtis.
4. *My next step is...*—kitas veiksmas.

**Model:** *I've completed the customer list. I'm checking the contact details now. The main problem is that five phone numbers are old. My next step is to ask the sales team for updated information by Wednesday.*

**Choose:** 1. *I have completed / I completed yesterday* the first draft. 2. *I'm checking / I check now* the figures. 3. My next step *is contacting / is to contact* the supplier.

**Your task:** pateikite 45 sekundžių pranešimą apie tikrą ar išgalvotą užduotį.  
**Checklist:** visi keturi etapai; dabartinė kliūtis; konkretus terminas.

## Workday 7 — Asking for Clarification / Pasitikslinimas

| Need | Phrase |
|---|---|
| repeat | *Could you say that again, please?* |
| slower | *Could you speak a little more slowly?* |
| meaning | *What does “dispatch” mean here?* |
| example | *Could you give me an example?* |
| confirm | *So you need it by Tuesday morning, correct?* |

**Bad strategy:** tylėti ir spėti.  
**Useful strategy:** pasakyti, kuri dalis neaiški, ir patvirtinti suprastą informaciją.

**Repair:** 1. *Repeat.* 2. *What means dispatch?* 3. *You need Tuesday yes?*

**Your task:** vienas partneris pateikia tris instrukcijas; kitas turi paprašyti pakartoti, paaiškinti vieną žodį ir patvirtinti terminą.  
**Checklist:** trys skirtingos pasitikslinimo frazės; mandagus tonas; terminas pakartotas.

---

## Workday 8 — A Deadline Problem / Problema dėl termino

**Model message**

> Hi Greta, I'm working on the monthly report, but I won't be able to finish it by 2 p.m. because the final sales figures arrived late. I can send a complete version by 4:30. If you need something earlier, I can send the finished sections at two. Which option would be more useful?

**Why it works / Kodėl veikia:** problema pranešama prieš terminą; yra priežastis; pateikiami du sprendimai; klausiama, kuris naudingesnis.

**Choose the professional option:**

1. **Report late. / I won't be able to finish the report by two.**
2. **Not my fault. / The final figures arrived later than expected.**
3. **Wait. / I can send the complete version by 4:30.**

**Your task:** praneškite, kad 10:00 terminas neįmanomas; pasiūlykite naują laiką ir dalinį variantą.  
**Checklist:** išankstinis pranešimas; faktinė priežastis; du sprendimai; mandagus klausimas.

---

## Workday 9 — Reporting a Practical Problem / Praktinės problemos pranešimas

**Fact ladder / Faktų seka:**

- location: *The printer in room 4...*
- exact problem: *...is showing error 18.*
- effect: *We cannot print delivery labels.*
- attempted action: *I have restarted it twice.*
- request: *Could someone check it before 11?*

**Quick repair:** 1. *Printer don't work.* 2. *We cannot to print.* 3. *I restarted already two times.*

**Your task:** praneškite apie neveikiantį liftą, internetą, kasos aparatą arba durų kortelę.  
**Checklist:** tiksli vieta; matomas faktas, ne spėjama diagnozė; poveikis; bandymas; laiku apibrėžtas prašymas.

## Workday 10 — Handling a Customer Request / Kliento prašymas

**Model dialogue**

> **Customer:** Could you deliver the order before Friday?  
> **Employee:** Let me check. The earliest available delivery is Friday morning.  
> **Customer:** Is collection possible on Thursday?  
> **Employee:** Yes. You can collect it after three. Would you like me to arrange that?  
> **Customer:** Yes, please. That would be helpful.

**Useful pattern:** patvirtinkite poreikį → patikrinkite → aiškiai pasakykite ribą → pasiūlykite alternatyvą → patvirtinkite susitarimą.

**Repair:** 1. *Earliest delivery it is Friday.* 2. *You want I arrange?* 3. *Yes, this will helpful.*

**Your task:** klientas nori neįmanomo laiko; pasiūlykite vieną realią alternatyvą.  
**Checklist:** be tuščio pažado; konkretus laikas; alternatyva; galutinis patvirtinimas.

---

## Workday 11 — Absence or Late Arrival / Neatvykimas arba vėlavimas

**Short message model**

> Hi Marius, I'm unwell this morning and won't be able to come to the office. I have told the reception team about today's visitor, and the notes are in the shared folder. I'll update you by 2 p.m. about tomorrow. Sorry for the short notice.

**Do include / Įtraukite:** darbo poveikį ir būtiną perdavimą.  
**Do not include / Nebūtina:** išsamią privačią medicininę informaciją.

**Choose:** 1. *I won't can come / I won't be able to come.* 2. *I told already / I have already told* the team. 3. I'll update you *until / by* two.

**Your task:** 40–60 žodžių žinutė apie neatvykimą arba 30 minučių vėlavimą.  
**Checklist:** dabartinė situacija; kas perduota; kada atnaujinsite informaciją; pagarba privatumui.

---

## Workday 12 — Giving Helpful Feedback / Naudingas grįžtamasis ryšys

**Supportive pattern:**

1. *The instructions were easy to follow.*—konkretus teigiamas dalykas.
2. *One detail was unclear.*—viena problema.
3. *Could you add an example here?*—konkretus pasiūlymas.

**Avoid:** *It's bad / I don't like it / Everything is wrong.*

**Model:** *The presentation had a clear structure, and the examples were useful. One chart was difficult to read because the text was small. Could you make that text larger before the customer meeting?*

**Your task:** pateikite grįžtamąjį ryšį apie el. laišką, instrukciją arba trumpą pristatymą.  
**Checklist:** vienas konkretus pliusas; viena problema; vienas veiksmas; pagarbus tonas.

---

## Workday 13 — Remote Work Check-In / Nuotolinio darbo susitarimas

**Model dialogue**

> **A:** Are you working from home tomorrow?  
> **B:** Yes. I'll be online from eight thirty. I have a video call at ten, and I'm going to finish the order list after lunch.  
> **A:** Could you also check the shared inbox before noon?  
> **B:** Yes. I'll message you when it's done.

**Repair:** 1. *I will online from eight.* 2. *I have video call on ten.* 3. *I message when finish.*

**Your task:** suderinkite nuotolinę dieną: pasiekiamumo laikas, du darbai, vienas skambutis ir užbaigimo žinutė.  
**Checklist:** *online from... to...*; *at + time*; du ateities modeliai; aiškus patvirtinimas.

---

## Workday 14 — Safety Instructions / Saugos nurodymai

**Notice**

> Visitors must sign in at reception and wear a visitor badge. You mustn't enter the warehouse without a staff member. In an emergency, use the nearest marked exit. Do not use the lift. Meet beside the blue sign in the car park.

**Questions:** 1. Where must visitors sign in? 2. What must they wear? 3. When is warehouse entry allowed? 4. Which exit should people use? 5. Where is the meeting point?

**Your task:** žodžiu paaiškinkite taisykles naujam lankytojui ir paprašykite jo pakartoti dvi svarbiausias.  
**Checklist:** pareiga; draudimas; avarinis veiksmas; susitikimo vieta; supratimo patikrinimas.

## Workday 15 — Welcoming a Visitor / Lankytojo priėmimas

| Step | Phrase |
|---|---|
| welcome | *Good morning. How can I help?* |
| identify | *Who are you here to see?* |
| check | *Could I have your name, please?* |
| update | *Ms Jankauskaitė will be with you in five minutes.* |
| comfort | *Please take a seat. Would you like some water?* |

**Repair:** 1. *Who you came to see?* 2. *Give name please.* 3. *She comes after five minutes.*

**Your task:** lankytojas atvyko dešimt minučių per anksti; priimkite jį, patikrinkite vardą ir pasiūlykite palaukti.  
**Checklist:** mandagus pasveikinimas; vardas; laukimo laikas; vieta; pasiūlymas.

## Workday 16 — Mini Workplace Project / Mažas darbo projektas

**Brief / Užduotis:** suplanuokite 30 minučių naujo darbuotojo priėmimą.

Paruoškite:

1. tikslą;
2. datą, laiką ir vietą;
3. tris trumpas veiklas;
4. kas už ką atsakingas;
5. kokios medžiagos reikia;
6. vieną galimą problemą ir Plan B;
7. 60–90 sekundžių žodinį pristatymą;
8. 50–70 žodžių patvirtinimo el. laišką.

**Model presentation:** *The aim is to give our new colleague a clear and friendly introduction. We'll meet in room 2 at nine on Monday. First, I'll introduce the team. Rasa will explain the daily schedule, and Mantas will show the main work areas. We need a visitor badge, a printed schedule, and a laptop. If room 2 is busy, we'll use the small training room instead.*

**Model email:** *Hi everyone, our new-colleague welcome session is on Monday at 9 a.m. in room 2. I'll introduce the team, Rasa will explain the schedule, and Mantas will show the work areas. Please bring the printed schedule and visitor badge. If room 2 is unavailable, we'll meet in the training room. Thanks!*

**Checklist:** visi aštuoni punktai; realūs laikai; aiškios atsakomybės; Plan B; mandagus užbaigimas.
