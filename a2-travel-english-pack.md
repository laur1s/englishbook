# A2 Travel English Pack
## A2 anglų kalba kelionėse

12 praktinių kelionės etapų: nuo maršruto ir bilieto iki viešbučio, pasikeitusio reiso, pamesto daikto ir skubios pagalbos. Kiekviename etape yra realistiškas dokumentas ar pokalbis, lietuviškas paaiškinimas, taisymo pratimas ir aiški kalbėjimo arba rašymo užduotis.

### Kelionės komunikacijos taisyklė

Kai jaudinatės, trumpinkite sakinį ir pateikite informaciją tokia tvarka:

**where → when → what happened → what you need**  
**kur → kada → kas atsitiko → ko reikia**

---

## Journey 1 — Reading a Timetable / Tvarkaraščio skaitymas

| Service | From | To | Departs | Arrives | Platform |
|---|---|---|---:|---:|---:|
| IC 204 | Vilnius | Šiauliai | 08:15 | 10:32 | 3 |
| IC 118 | Vilnius | Klaipėda | 09:05 | 13:12 | 5 |
| R 602 | Vilnius | Kaunas | 09:20 | 10:38 | 1 |
| IC 310 | Vilnius | Riga | 10:10 | 14:24 | 4 |

**Questions / Klausimai:**

1. Which service leaves first?
2. How long does the Kaunas journey take?
3. Which train uses platform 5?
4. When does the Riga train arrive?
5. You need to be in Šiauliai before 11:00. Which service can you take?

**Speak:** *The train to ___ leaves at ___ from platform ___. It arrives at ___.*  
**LT pagalba:** sakykite *at 09:05*, *from platform 5*, *arrive in Klaipėda*, bet *arrive at the station*.

---

## Journey 2 — Buying the Right Ticket / Tinkamo bilieto pirkimas

**Your need:** Vilnius → Kaunas, travel Friday, return Sunday, one adult, second class.

**Model dialogue**

> **Clerk:** How can I help?  
> **Passenger:** I'd like a return ticket to Kaunas, please.  
> **Clerk:** When are you coming back?  
> **Passenger:** On Sunday evening. Is the 18:40 train direct?  
> **Clerk:** Yes. Would you like first or second class?  
> **Passenger:** Second class, please. Can I choose a seat?

**Repair:** 1. *I want ticket until Kaunas.* 2. *I come back in Sunday.* 3. *Can I choose place?*

**Your task:** pakeiskite miestą, dienas ir grįžimo laiką.  
**Checklist:** *single/return*; *to + city*; *on + day*; klasė; vienas papildomas klausimas.

---

## Journey 3 — Understanding an Announcement / Pranešimas stotyje

**Announcement**

> Attention, please. The 10:10 service to Riga is delayed by approximately thirty minutes because of a technical problem. It will now depart from platform 6, not platform 4. Passengers for Šiauliai should still use this train. We apologise for the delay.

**Choose and answer:**

1. The train is **cancelled / delayed / early**.
2. The delay is about **13 / 30 / 40** minutes.
3. The new platform is ___.
4. What caused the change?
5. Should passengers for Šiauliai choose another train?

**Repair phrase bank:** *Did you say platform six? How long is the delay? Is my ticket still valid?*  
**LT pagalba:** išgirdę svarbų skaičių, jį pakartokite klausimu—tai ne nemandagumas, o saugus pasitikslinimas.

## Journey 4 — Airport Check-In / Registracija oro uoste

| English | Lietuviška reikšmė |
|---|---|
| I'd like to check in for the flight to Copenhagen. | Norėčiau užsiregistruoti skrydžiui į Kopenhagą. |
| Do you have any bags to check in? | Ar turite registruojamo bagažo? |
| I only have hand luggage. | Turiu tik rankinį bagažą. |
| Could I have an aisle seat? | Ar galėčiau gauti vietą prie praėjimo? |
| What time does boarding start? | Kada prasideda įlaipinimas? |

**Repair:** 1. *I check in to Copenhagen flight.* 2. *I have only hand baggage one.* 3. *When boarding starts?*

**Role-play:** keliautojas turi vieną registruojamą lagaminą, nori vietos prie lango ir klausia vartų bei įlaipinimo laiko.  
**Checklist:** kelionės tikslas; bagažas; vietos pageidavimas; vartai; laikas.

## Journey 5 — Security and Signs / Saugumo patikra ir ženklai

Match the sign to its meaning.

1. **Boarding passes and ID ready**  
2. **Liquids: containers up to 100 ml**  
3. **Remove laptops from bags**  
4. **No entry — staff only**  
5. **Gate closes 20 minutes before departure**

a. Įėjimas tik darbuotojams.  
b. Išimkite nešiojamus kompiuterius.  
c. Vartai uždaromi likus 20 minučių.  
d. Paruoškite dokumentus.  
e. Skysčių talpos negali viršyti nurodyto dydžio.

**Ask safely:** *Do I need to remove my tablet? Can I take this bottle through? Where should I put my jacket?*  
**LT pagalba:** taisyklę aiškinkite su *must/have to/mustn't*, o klausimą—su *Do I need to...? Can I...?*

## Journey 6 — Checking into Accommodation / Apgyvendinimas

**Booking**

> **Name:** Eglė Petrauskienė  
> **Room:** double room  
> **Dates:** 14–17 August  
> **Guests:** 2  
> **Breakfast:** included  
> **Check-out:** 11:00

**Reception questions:**

1. What surname is the booking under?
2. How many nights is the stay?
3. Is breakfast extra?
4. When must the guests check out?
5. Which two practical questions could the guest still ask?

**Model:** *Hello, I have a booking under Petrauskienė. It's a double room for three nights. Could you tell me where breakfast is served? Also, is it possible to leave our bags after check-out?*

**LT pagalba:** rezervacija yra *under + pavardė*; nakvynių skaičių skaičiuokite tarp atvykimo ir išvykimo datų.

## Journey 7 — A Room Problem / Problema kambaryje

**Useful structure:** room number → exact fact → effect → request.

**Model call**

> Hello, I'm calling from room 308. The air conditioning is making a very loud noise, and we cannot sleep. I switched it off, but the room is becoming very hot. Could someone check it, or could we move to another quiet room?

**Choose the factual sentence:**

1. **The room is terrible. / The air conditioning is making a loud noise.**
2. **Nothing works. / I switched it off, but the room became hot.**
3. **Fix it. / Could someone check it, please?**

**Your task:** pasirinkite problemą: nėra karšto vandens, neveikia raktas, triukšmas arba nešvari patalynė.  
**Checklist:** kambario numeris; matomas faktas; poveikis; kas jau bandyta; du galimi sprendimai.

## Journey 8 — Eating Out While Travelling / Maitinimas kelionėje

**Menu extract**

| Dish | Notes | Price |
|---|---|---:|
| vegetable soup | contains cream | €6 |
| grilled chicken | served with rice | €13 |
| mushroom pasta | vegetarian | €11 |
| apple cake | contains nuts | €5 |

**Questions:** 1. Which main dish is vegetarian? 2. Which two dishes contain a stated ingredient? 3. What is the total for soup and pasta? 4. Which dessert must a person avoiding nuts not order?

**Model order:** *Could I have the mushroom pasta, please? Does it contain any nuts? Also, could we have some tap water?*

**LT pagalba:** apie alergiją ar svarbų ingredientą klauskite tiesiai: *Does it contain...? I need to avoid...* Jei sveikatai svarbu, paprašykite darbuotojo patvirtinti.

## Journey 9 — Finding a Place / Vietos paieška

**Starting point:** central square.  
**Destination:** travel information office.  
**Route facts:** cross the square → pass the town hall → turn right at the bank → office beside the post office, opposite a bus stop.

**Task A:** iš faktų sukurkite 4–5 žingsnių kelio paaiškinimą.  
**Task B:** sukurkite du pasitikslinimo klausimus.  
**Task C:** pasakykite maršrutą atgal į centrinę aikštę.

**Model:** *Cross the square and walk past the town hall. Turn right at the bank. The information office is beside the post office, opposite a bus stop. It is about five minutes from here.*

**Checklist:** veiksmažodžiai; vietos prielinksniai; orientyras; atstumas arba trukmė; supratimo patikrinimas.

## Journey 10 — Changing a Booking / Rezervacijos keitimas

**Original booking:** two nights, 5–7 October, single room.  
**Needed change:** arrive 6 October, leave 9 October, same room type.

**Model email**

> **Subject: Date change for booking KP481**  
> Hello, I have a single-room booking from 5 to 7 October under the name Kazlauskas. My travel dates have changed. Is it possible to move the booking to 6–9 October and keep the same room type? Please let me know whether the price will change. Thank you.

**Repair:** 1. *I have booking from 5 until 7.* 2. *My travel dates changed.* 3. *Price will change?*

**Your task:** pakeiskite datas, svečių skaičių arba kambario tipą.  
**Checklist:** užsakymo numeris; sena informacija; nauja informacija; kainos klausimas; patvirtinimo prašymas.

## Journey 11 — Lost Property / Pamestas daiktas

**Description grid**

| Detail | Example |
|---|---|
| object | small backpack |
| colour/material | dark green, nylon |
| identifying feature | yellow key ring on front zip |
| contents | guidebook, glasses case, charger |
| last place/time | bus 12, around 16:30 |

**Model report:** *I've lost a small dark-green nylon backpack. It has a yellow key ring on the front zip. There is a guidebook, a glasses case, and a charger inside. I last saw it on bus 12 at about 16:30. Has anyone handed it in?*

**Repair:** 1. *I lost small green backpack.* 2. *Inside are charger.* 3. *Last time I see it in bus.*

**Your task:** apibūdinkite kitą daiktą penkiomis kategorijomis.  
**Checklist:** artikelis; trys išskirtinės detalės; turinys be jautrių duomenų; paskutinė vieta ir laikas; kontaktinis klausimas.

---

## Journey 12 — Urgent Travel Help / Skubi pagalba kelionėje

**Possible situations:** pasas dingo; žmogus nukrito; pavojingoje vietoje paliktas krepšys; atsiskyrėte nuo vaiko ar grupės.

**Communication order:**

1. *I need urgent help.*
2. exact place + landmark;
3. visible facts only;
4. number of people;
5. immediate danger, if any;
6. *Please tell me what to do next.*

**Model:** *I need urgent help at Central Station, beside platform 2. My ten-year-old son became separated from me about five minutes ago. He is wearing a red jacket and carrying a blue backpack. I am waiting beside the information desk. Please tell me what to do next.*

**Your task:** pasirinkite vieną situaciją ir pateikite 30–45 sekundžių aiškų pranešimą.  
**Checklist:** tiksli vieta; patikrinti faktai; laikas; išvaizdos ar daikto aprašymas; vykdyti oficialių darbuotojų nurodymus.

# Travel Answer and Repair Key / Kelionių atsakymai

## Journey 1

## Journey 3
1. delayed 2. 30 3. platform 6 4. a technical problem 5. No; they should still use this train.

## Journey 4

## Journey 5
1–d, 2–e, 3–b, 4–a, 5–c.

## Journey 6
1. Petrauskienė. 2. Three nights. 3. No, it is included. 4. At 11:00. 5. Galimi klausimai: apie pusryčių vietą, internetą, bagažą, transportą ar kambario vietą.

## Journey 7
1. The air conditioning is making a loud noise. 2. I switched it off, but the room became hot. 3. Could someone check it, please?

## Journey 8
1. Mushroom pasta. 2. Vegetable soup and apple cake. 3. €17. 4. Apple cake, because the menu says that it contains nuts. Jei alergija rimta, apie kitus patiekalus ir kryžminį užteršimą klauskite darbuotojo.

## Journey 10
1. I have **a** booking from 5 **to** 7 October. 2. My travel dates **have** changed. 3. **Will the** price change?

## Journey 11
1. I have lost **a** small green backpack. 2. Inside **is a** charger. / There is a charger inside. 3. I last **saw** it **on the** bus.
