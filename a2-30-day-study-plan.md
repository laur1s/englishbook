# A2 30-Day Lithuanian-Supported Study Plan
## A2 30 dienų mokymosi planas su lietuviška pagalba

Šis planas sujungia 24 kurso skyrius ir naujus praktikos rinkinius į aiškią 30 dienų seką. Vienai dienai skirkite **25–40 minučių**. Jei turite tik 15 minučių, atlikite žvaigždute pažymėtus veiksmus.

### Kasdienė formulė

- ⭐ **Learn / Suprask:** 8–12 min. Perskaitykite tikslinę pamokos dalį.
- ⭐ **Use / Pavartok:** 8–10 min. Atlikite nurodytą pratimą.
- **Hear / Išgirsk:** 5 min. Skaityk–uždenk–atkurk arba partnerio skaitymas.
- ⭐ **Speak / Pasakyk:** 2–5 min. Įrašas arba pokalbis.
- **Repair / Ištaisyk:** 3 min. Viena klaida → priežastis lietuviškai → naujas pavyzdys.

### Jei praleidote dieną

Nedvigubinkite kitos dienos. Tęskite nuo praleistos dienos arba atlikite tik tris žvaigždute pažymėtus veiksmus. Tikslas—pastovus grįžimas, ne tobula kalendoriaus grandinė.

## Week 1 — Everyday Foundations / Kasdieniai pagrindai

### Day 1 — Introduce Yourself / Prisistatykite

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 1](/a2/unit-01): **to be**, greetings, personal information.
2. ⭐ Do [Practice Workbook Exercise 1](/resources/a2-practice-workbook#exercise-1--meeting-a-new-neighbour).
3. Hear: [Listening Day 1](/resources/a2-listening-dictation-pack#day-1--a-new-colleague-nauja-kolegė).
4. ⭐ Record 30 seconds: name, town, work/study, one interest.
5. Repair: patikrinkite **am/is/are** ir klausimo tvarką.

### Day 2 — A Real Weekday / Tikra darbo diena

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 2](/a2/unit-02): Present Simple and frequency.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 2.
3. Hear: Listening Day 2.
4. ⭐ Tell your partner or phone what you usually do before 9 a.m.
5. Repair: su **he/she/it** patikrinkite **-s**.

### Day 3 — Tell a Past Story / Papasakokite praeities istoriją

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 3](/a2/unit-03): Past Simple.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 3.
3. Hear: Listening Day 3.
4. ⭐ Tell a six-sentence story with *first, then, suddenly, in the end*.
5. Repair: išsirinkite tris netaisyklingus veiksmažodžius ir sukurkite naujus sakinius.

### Day 4 — Food and Shopping / Maistas ir pirkiniai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 4](/a2/unit-04): countable/uncountable nouns and shopping.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 4.
3. Hear: Listening Day 4.
4. ⭐ Plan a simple dinner and say the quantities aloud.
5. Repair: paaiškinkite lietuviškai skirtumą tarp **many** ir **much**.

### Day 5 — Directions / Kelio nurodymai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 5](/a2/unit-05): directions and place prepositions.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 5.
3. Hear: Listening Day 5.
4. ⭐ Give directions from your home to one useful place.
5. Repair: patikrinkite *at the lights, past the bank, opposite the library*.

### Day 6 — Plans and Backup Plans / Planai ir atsarginiai planai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 6](/a2/unit-06): *going to, will*, arrangements.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 6.
3. Hear: Listening Day 6.
4. ⭐ Plan Saturday and add one weather backup.
5. Repair: pažymėkite, kuri forma reiškia susitarimą, ketinimą ir spontanišką sprendimą.

### Day 7 — Week 1 Retrieval / 1 savaitės prisiminimas

1. ⭐ Do Vocabulary Games 1, 5, and 17 from the [Game Bank](/resources/a2-vocabulary-game-bank).
2. ⭐ Without notes, say one useful sentence from each Unit 1–6 topic.
3. Complete Workbook Bonus Exercises 25–27.
4. With a partner: Couples Conversation 5. Solo: record both roles.
5. Repair only the three mistakes that most affected meaning.

## Week 2 — Time, Experience, and Choice / Laikas, patirtis ir pasirinkimai

### Day 8 — Life Experience / Gyvenimo patirtis

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 7](/a2/unit-07): Present Perfect introduction.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 7.
3. Hear: Listening Day 7.
4. ⭐ Ask three *Have you ever...?* questions and add Past Simple follow-ups.
5. Repair: konkretus baigtas laikas → Past Simple.

### Day 9 — Compare Options / Palyginkite variantus

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 8](/a2/unit-08): comparatives and superlatives.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 8.
3. Hear: Listening Day 8.
4. ⭐ Compare two shops, routes, phones, or places to stay.
5. Repair: ne *more cheaper*; rinkitės vieną palyginimo formą.

### Day 10 — Advice and Rules / Patarimai ir taisyklės

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 9](/a2/unit-09): modal verbs.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 9.
3. Hear: Listening Day 9.
4. ⭐ Give one suggestion, obligation, prohibition, and optional action.
5. Repair: garsiai paaiškinkite **mustn't** ir **don't have to** skirtumą.

### Day 11 — Habit or Now? / Įprotis ar dabar?

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 10](/a2/unit-10): Present Simple vs Present Continuous.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 10.
3. Hear: Listening Day 10.
4. ⭐ Say three usual actions and three actions happening now.
5. Repair: būsenos veiksmažodžiai, pvz., *know/need/like*, dažniausiai nevartojami Continuous formoje.

### Day 12 — Background and Interruption / Fonas ir įvykis

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 11](/a2/unit-11): Past Continuous.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 11.
3. Hear: Listening Day 11.
4. ⭐ Build one story with *was/were ...ing when...*.
5. Repair: pažymėkite foninį veiksmą ir jį nutraukusį įvykį.

### Day 13 — A2.2 Checkpoint / A2.2 tarpinis patikrinimas

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 12](/a2/unit-12) and complete one mixed section.
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 12 without the key.
3. Hear: Listening Day 12.
4. ⭐ Speak for one minute: yesterday, one life experience, one future plan.
5. Use the [Lithuanian Error Clinic](/resources/a2-lithuanian-error-clinic) for your two most common mistakes.

### Day 14 — Week 2 Retrieval / 2 savaitės prisiminimas

1. ⭐ Do Game Bank Games 15, 22, and 23.
2. ⭐ Complete Bonus Exercises 28–31.
3. Read one dialogue from the [Real-Life Phrasebook](/resources/a2-real-life-phrasebook), cover it, and rebuild it.
4. Partner: Couples Conversation 8. Solo: ask and answer both roles.
5. Write five forms you want to repeat next week.

## Week 3 — Objects, People, Home, and Work / Daiktai, žmonės, namai ir darbas

### Day 15 — Objects and Determiners / Daiktai ir pažymimieji žodžiai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 13](/a2/unit-13).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 13.
3. Hear: Listening Day 13.
4. ⭐ Describe a lost object without saying its name.
5. Repair: *my/your* prieš daiktavardį; *mine/yours* be daiktavardžio.

### Day 16 — Pronouns and Relationships / Įvardžiai ir santykiai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 14](/a2/unit-14).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 14.
3. Hear: Listening Day 14.
4. ⭐ Describe an important person in five sentences.
5. Repair: kas atlieka veiksmą → subject pronoun; ką veiksmas pasiekia → object pronoun.

### Day 17 — Home Responsibilities / Namų pareigos

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 15](/a2/unit-15).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 15.
3. Hear: Listening Day 15.
4. ⭐ Complete Couples Conversation 2 or record your own house meeting.
5. Repair: *has to* teiginyje, bet *doesn't have to* neiginyje.

### Day 18 — Health Communication / Bendravimas apie sveikatą

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 16](/a2/unit-16).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 16.
3. Hear: Listening Day 16.
4. ⭐ Practise Service 4 from the [Services Pack](/resources/a2-services-english-pack).
5. Repair: simptomai + trukmė + saugus klausimas; nebandykite savarankiškai nustatyti diagnozės.

### Day 19 — Weather Decisions / Sprendimai pagal orą

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 17](/a2/unit-17).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 17.
3. Hear: Listening Day 17.
4. ⭐ Give today's forecast, suitable clothing, and one backup plan.
5. Repair: mokykitės visų junginių—*heavy rain, strong wind, thick fog*.

### Day 20 — Work and Learning / Darbas ir mokymasis

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 18](/a2/unit-18).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 18.
3. Hear: Listening Day 18.
4. ⭐ Complete Workplace Pack Workday 1 or 6.
5. Repair: *good at + -ing; want/need to + verb*.

### Day 21 — Week 3 Retrieval / 3 savaitės prisiminimas

1. ⭐ Do Game Bank Games 2, 11, and 26.
2. ⭐ Complete Bonus Exercises 32–35.
3. Partner: Couples Conversations 9 or 17. Solo: record a two-part answer.
4. Review five words from Days 15–20 with Game 29.
5. Choose one corrected sentence to keep on your phone or notebook.

## Week 4 — Technology, Social Life, Services, and Projects / Technologijos, bendravimas ir projektai

### Day 22 — Technology Help / Techninė pagalba

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 19](/a2/unit-19).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 19.
3. Hear: Listening Day 19.
4. ⭐ Explain one problem, two attempted actions, and the help you need.
5. Repair: *sign in, attach a file, save work, update an app*.

### Day 23 — Invitations and Changes / Kvietimai ir pakeitimai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 20](/a2/unit-20).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 20.
3. Hear: Listening Day 20.
4. ⭐ Create a six-message invitation and change dialogue.
5. Repair: *would like to join; how about meeting; let's meet*.

### Day 24 — Environment and Results / Aplinka ir rezultatai

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 21](/a2/unit-21).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 21.
3. Hear: Listening Day 21.
4. ⭐ Suggest one small home or workplace environmental change.
5. Repair: *fewer cups, less waste; If people use..., we will...*

### Day 25 — Solve a Service Problem / Išspręskite paslaugos problemą

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 22](/a2/unit-22).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 22.
3. Hear: Listening Day 22.
4. ⭐ Complete Services Pack Service 8, 9, or 12.
5. Repair: faktas → lūkestis → prašymas → padėka.

### Day 26 — Describe People, Places, and Things / Apibūdinkite

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 23](/a2/unit-23).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 23.
3. Hear: Listening Day 23.
4. ⭐ Recommend a person **who**, a place **where**, and a thing **which**.
5. Repair: santykinis sakinys turi eiti iškart po apibūdinamo žodžio.

### Day 27 — Final Communication Project / Baigiamasis projektas

1. ⭐ Open [Unit 24](/a2/unit-24).
2. ⭐ Do Workbook Exercise 24.
3. Hear: Listening Day 24.
4. ⭐ Prepare a 60–90 second real-life project and a 50–70 word message.
5. Repair only three language points that most affect clarity.

### Day 28 — Real-Life Rotation / Realių situacijų ratas

1. ⭐ Choose one travel situation from the [Travel Pack](/resources/a2-travel-english-pack).
2. ⭐ Choose one workplace situation from the [Workplace Pack](/resources/a2-workplace-english-pack).
3. Choose one services situation.
4. Speak for 45 seconds in each situation.
5. Repeat the weakest situation after checking the model.

## Final Two Days — Evidence and Next Steps / Įrodymai ir kiti žingsniai

### Day 29 — Personal A2 Challenge / Asmeninis A2 iššūkis

Complete five tasks without notes:

1. ⭐ introduce yourself and describe a normal day;
2. ⭐ tell a past story;
3. compare two options and recommend one;
4. solve a service problem politely;
5. explain one future plan and backup.

**Self-check / Įsivertinimas:**

- Ar pagrindinė mintis buvo aiški?
- Ar pateikiau reikalingas detales?
- Ar galėjau pasitaisyti arba paprašyti paaiškinimo?
- Kurios trys formos dar reikalauja kartojimo?

### Day 30 — Build Your Next Month / Susikurkite kitą mėnesį

1. ⭐ Repeat the Day 1 introduction and compare both versions.
2. ⭐ Choose four strong topics and four developing topics.
3. For each developing topic, choose one lesson, one workbook exercise, and one game.
4. Schedule two 15-minute couple conversations or solo recordings per week.
5. Write this commitment:

> *For the next four weeks, I will practise English on ___, ___, and ___. My shortest session will be 15 minutes. I will repeat mistakes after two days, and I will keep one useful sentence from every session.*
