## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- Is this notebook yours? — Ar šis sąsiuvinis tavo?
- No, mine is on the table. — Ne, manasis yra ant stalo.
- I think it is hers/his. — Manau, tai jos / jo.
- These keys are ours. — Šie raktai yra mūsų.
- Please give it to him. — Prašau, paduok jį jam.
- Please give them to her. — Prašau, paduok juos jai.
- Whose bag is this? — Kieno čia krepšys?

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Keep the objects visible in your imagination and make every reference clear. Say the noun first when a listener may not know what **it**, **one**, or **hers** refers to.

Your exchange succeeds when all four objects reach a clear owner and you correct two guesses politely. On the second attempt, change two owners so you must adapt the pronouns. Check that possessive pronouns stand alone (**This is mine**) and object pronouns follow a verb or preposition (**Give it to her**).
