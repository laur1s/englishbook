## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

Keep the objects visible in your imagination and make every reference clear. Say the noun first when a listener may not know what **it**, **one**, or **hers** refers to.

On the second attempt, change two owners. Check that possessive pronouns stand alone (**This is mine**) and object pronouns follow a verb or preposition (**Give it to her**).
