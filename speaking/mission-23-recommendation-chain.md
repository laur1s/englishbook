## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

Specific details make a recommendation more useful than general praise. Instead of saying a place is simply “nice,” explain what a visitor can do there or why it suits a particular person.

Use **who** for a person, **where** for a place, and **that/which** for a thing. On the second attempt, make all three reasons different and concrete.
