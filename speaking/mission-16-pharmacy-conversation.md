## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

This mission practises communication, not medical diagnosis. Use only mild example symptoms, avoid promising that a treatment will work, and recommend professional or emergency help for serious warning signs.

During the second attempt, ask one clear checking question and keep the advice short. The goal is to explain, check, and respond safely in everyday English.
