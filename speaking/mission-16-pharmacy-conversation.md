## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- I've had ... for two days. — ... turiu jau dvi dienas.
- How long have you had it? — Kiek laiko tai tęsiasi?
- Do you have any other symptoms? — Ar turite kitų simptomų?
- You can ... to feel more comfortable. — Galite..., kad pasijustumėte geriau.
- Please follow the instructions. — Laikykitės instrukcijų.
- If it gets worse, contact a doctor. — Jei pablogėtų, kreipkitės į gydytoją.
- If you cannot breathe or speak normally, call 112. — Jei negalite normaliai kvėpuoti ar kalbėti, skambinkite 112.
- If it is an emergency, call 112. — Jei tai skubi situacija, skambinkite 112.

## Safety and Success Check / Saugumas ir sėkmės kriterijai

This mission practises communication, not medical diagnosis. Use only mild example symptoms, avoid promising that a treatment will work, and recommend professional help if symptoms get worse. If a person is gasping or cannot speak normally because they cannot breathe, treat it as an emergency and call 112.

Your exchange succeeds when the customer describes the symptom and duration, the pharmacist asks at least one checking question, and the advice is cautious. During the second attempt, change the mild symptom, ask a different checking question, and clearly separate routine help from urgent help.
