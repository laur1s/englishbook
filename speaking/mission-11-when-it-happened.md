Good storytelling depends on the contrast between the scene and the events, not on making every verb continuous.

## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- I was... when... — Aš... kai...
- While we were... — Kol mes...
- Suddenly... — Staiga...
- Then I... — Tada aš...
- Luckily / Unfortunately... — Laimei / Deja...

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Your story succeeds when it sets a scene with the Past Continuous, interrupts it with a clear Past Simple event, and explains the result. On the second attempt, add one simultaneous background action and make the turning point easy to hear without exaggerating your speed.

## Push It Further / Pasunkinkite

- Add two simultaneous background actions.
- Ask a partner what they were doing at the same time.
- Retell the story from another person's point of view and change **was** to **were** where needed.
