This mission pushes Unit 7 into real speech. The learner has to manage meaning first and grammar second: what is my experience, and when do I need a finished past detail?

## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- I've been to...
- I've never...
- I've already...
- I did that in...
- It was...

## Push It Further / Pasunkinkite

- Ask yourself one ever question and answer it.
- Add since or for in one sentence.
- Repeat the mission and reduce the number of pauses.
