## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

Success means a clear, responsive plan—not perfect grammar or memorised speech. Keep the plan within the stated budget and respond to the guest's food, transport, and mobility needs.

Use the first recording to find one weak section. During the second attempt, repeat only that section and make the choice, reason, and backup easier for a listener to follow.
