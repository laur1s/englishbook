## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- I'll meet you at... — Pasitiksiu tave...
- The ... is cheaper, but the ... is easier. — ... pigesnis, bet ... patogesnis.
- It will cost about... — Tai kainuos apie...
- Would you prefer ... or...? — Ar labiau norėtum... ar...?
- If it rains, we'll... instead. — Jei lis, vietoj to mes...
- The ... is closed, so let's... — ... uždaryta, todėl...
- Sorry, could you repeat that? — Atsiprašau, ar galėtum pakartoti?
- Let me confirm the plan. — Dar kartą patvirtinsiu planą.

## Mission Success Check / Misijos sėkmės kriterijai

Success means a clear, responsive plan—not perfect grammar or memorised speech. Keep the plan within the stated budget and respond to the guest's food, transport, and mobility needs.

Your plan succeeds when it covers arrival, food, activities, budget, transport, and a backup; responds to both changes; offers the guest a real choice; and finishes with a clear confirmation. For the second attempt, use the first recording to find one weak section, change one guest need, and repeat only that section more clearly.
