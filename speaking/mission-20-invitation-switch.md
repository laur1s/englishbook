## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- Would you like to...? — Ar norėtum...?
- How about ...ing? — Gal...?
- I'd love to. — Labai norėčiau.
- Thanks, but I can't because... — Ačiū, bet negaliu, nes...
- Could we go on ... instead? — Gal galėtume eiti...?
- What time shall we meet? — Kada susitinkame?
- Let's meet at/in... — Susitikime...
- Great, see you then. — Puiku, iki pasimatymo.

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Polite social language helps both people continue the conversation. A useful refusal normally includes thanks, a short reason, and another possible time or activity.

Your exchange succeeds when one invitation is accepted, one is declined warmly with an alternative, and the accepted activity, place, date, and time are clear. On the second attempt, swap which invitation you accept and make the refusal sound warm without reading it.
