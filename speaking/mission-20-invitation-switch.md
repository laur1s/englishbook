## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

Polite social language helps both people continue the conversation. A useful refusal normally includes thanks, a short reason, and another possible time or activity.

On the second attempt, listen for the final details. Both speakers should know what they are doing, where they are meeting, and at what time.
