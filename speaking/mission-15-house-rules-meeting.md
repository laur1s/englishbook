## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

Aim for a fair agreement, not the strongest possible rules. **Don't have to** means an action is optional; **mustn't** means it is forbidden. Keep those meanings separate.

The second attempt should sound like a useful summary for new flatmates. Use a calm tone and make it clear who is responsible for each chore.
