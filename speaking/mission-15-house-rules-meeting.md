## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- We have to... — Turime...
- You don't have to... — Tau nereikia...
- We mustn't... — Negalima...
- Everyone can... — Visi gali...
- Could you take care of...? — Ar galėtum pasirūpinti...?
- I can do that on... — Galiu tai padaryti...
- That sounds fair. — Man tai atrodo sąžininga.
- Could we change one thing? — Ar galėtume vieną dalyką pakeisti?

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Aim for a fair agreement, not the strongest possible rules. **Don't have to** means an action is optional; **mustn't** means it is forbidden. Keep those meanings separate.

Your agreement succeeds when it includes one duty, one optional action, one permission, one prohibition, and a clear person or time for each chore. For the second attempt, swap one chore, disagree politely, and reach a new compromise before giving the four-rule summary.
