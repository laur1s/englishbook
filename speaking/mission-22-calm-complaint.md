## Mission notes / Misijos pastabos

A clear complaint gives the other person enough information to help. Describe the purchase and problem before asking for a repair, replacement, or refund.

Keep the situation ordinary and the request reasonable. On the second attempt, remove angry or vague language and finish by confirming exactly what will happen next.
