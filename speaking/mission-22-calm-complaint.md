## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- I bought this ... on... — Šį ... pirkau...
- I have the receipt. — Turiu kvitą.
- It is too... / It isn't ... enough. — Jis per... / Jis nepakankamai...
- It has stopped working. — Jis nustojo veikti.
- Could you help me, please? — Ar galėtumėte man padėti?
- We can repair it, replace it, or give you a refund. — Galime prekę pataisyti, pakeisti arba grąžinti jums pinigus.
- I'd prefer... — Norėčiau...
- So, what happens next? — Taigi, kas bus toliau?

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

A clear complaint gives the other person enough information to help. Describe the purchase and problem before asking for a repair, replacement, or refund.

Your exchange succeeds when it includes the purchase facts, a specific problem, a polite request, two possible solutions, and a confirmed next step. On the second attempt, change the product and preferred solution, then replace any angry or vague language with calm facts.
