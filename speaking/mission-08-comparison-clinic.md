This mission trains your ear. Many learners can fill in comparative tables, but they do not notice mistakes quickly when they need to speak.

## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- ...is bigger than... — ...yra didesnis / didesnė už...
- ...is more interesting than... — ...yra įdomesnis / įdomesnė už...
- ...is as quiet as... — ...yra toks pat ramus / tokia pat rami kaip...
- ...is the best... — ...yra geriausias / geriausia...
- I recommend... because... — Rekomenduoju..., nes...

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Your comparison succeeds when it corrects both original errors, compares two places in at least three ways, uses one superlative, and ends with a reasoned recommendation. On the second attempt, change one comparison and speak from three keywords rather than a script.

## Push It Further / Pasunkinkite

- Compare two restaurants, cities, or phones.
- Ask the listener which option they prefer and why.
