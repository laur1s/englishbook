The goal is a short, connected story—not a list of unrelated past-tense sentences.

## Useful Building Blocks / Naudingi sakiniai

- Last weekend, I... — Praėjusį savaitgalį aš...
- First..., then... — Pirmiausia..., tada...
- After that... — Po to...
- Finally... — Galiausiai...
- I felt... because... — Jaučiausi..., nes...

## Success Check / Sėkmės kriterijai

Your story succeeds when the listener can identify when and where it happened, follow at least three completed actions, and hear a clear ending or feeling. On the second attempt, use all four sequence words and add one sensory detail—what you saw, heard, ate, or felt.

## Push It Further / Pasunkinkite

- Add one negative sentence.
- Ask a partner, "What did you do next?"
- Retell the story in fewer words without losing its beginning, turning point, and ending.
