# Unit 17: Weather, Clothes, and Plans

## Learning Objectives / Mokymosi tikslai

- Understand a simple forecast and choose suitable clothes.
- Use Present Continuous for a fixed personal arrangement.
- Connect reasons and results with **because** and **so**.

## Language Focus / Kalbos pagrindai

Use Present Continuous for an arrangement with a time or place: *I'm meeting Ema at six.* Use **going to** for a plan or evidence-based prediction: *Those clouds are dark. It's going to rain.* **Because** introduces a reason; **so** introduces a result.

<p class="lt-text" lang="lt"><strong>Lietuviška atrama:</strong> Present Continuous su laiku ar vieta gali reikšti sutartą planą. <strong>Going to</strong> tinka planui arba prognozei, kai matome požymius. <strong>Because</strong> pateikia priežastį, o <strong>so</strong> – rezultatą.</p>

## Exercise 2: Choose the Future Meaning / Pratimas 2

1. I **am meeting / meet** Lina at the station at 8 tomorrow.
2. Look at the sky! It **is going to / is meeting** snow.
3. We bought tickets; we **are travelling / travel** on Friday.
4. It is cold, **because / so** take a warm coat.
5. I am wearing boots **because / so** the path is wet.
6. What time **are you leaving / do you leaving**?

## Exercise 3: Build Forecast Sentences / Pratimas 3

1. windy / coast / tomorrow 2. temperature / fall / evening 3. sunny / morning / cloudy / later 4. rain / so / umbrella

## Exercise 4: Read the Weekend Forecast / Pratimas 4

> Saturday morning will be dry and bright, but strong winds will arrive after lunch. The temperature will fall from 14°C to 8°C. Sunday will start cloudy, and heavy rain is likely in the afternoon. The hiking group is meeting at 9 a.m. on Saturday, so everyone should bring a windproof jacket.

1. When will the wind become strong?
2. How much will the temperature fall?
3. When is heavy rain likely?
4. What should hikers bring?

## Exercise 5: Solve the Packing Problem / Pratimas 5

Choose three useful items for the forecast: **sandals, windproof jacket, umbrella, thick winter coat, water bottle**. More than one choice can be sensible, so explain each answer with **because** or **so**.

## Exercise 6: Plan an Outdoor Day / Pratimas 6

Write your arrangement, forecast, clothes, and backup plan. Model: **We are meeting at the lake at ten. It is going to be warm in the morning, so I am wearing light clothes. Rain is possible later, so I am taking a jacket. If it rains heavily, we will visit the museum.**
